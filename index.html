Methodology

How we rank.

Every company on this page has been evaluated against the same six criteria. These are the areas that distinguish good companies from not-so-good, or downright bad. Rankings are updated quarterly.

1

Data Verification

Company-reported figures (carrier counts, insurance limits, years in operation) are cross-checked against public records; discrepancies count against the entry.

2

Pricing Transparency

Quote stability is the test. Brokers that lowball to win the booking and adjust at pickup are scored down, even when their headline rates look attractive.

3

Carrier Network and Vetting

How each broker screens carriers, what their dispatch failure rate looks like, and whether their network has the breadth to cover non-metro lanes.

4

Customer Experience and Communication

Pickup window accuracy, response time, and proactive status updates. Aggregated from third-party review platforms rather than self-reported case studies.

5

Complaint Patterns and Resolution

Complaint volume relative to shipment volume; resolution rates; how the company handles damage claims. Pattern matters more than raw count.

6

Long-Term Track Record

Years in operation weighted alongside the other criteria. Tenure is the strongest single correlate with consistent performance and delivery in this industry.
