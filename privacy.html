Privacy Policy

This page outlines how TopAutoShippingCompanies collects, uses, and protects information from visitors to this site.

When you submit a quote request or contact form, we collect the information you provide directly (name, email, phone, vehicle details, origin and destination). We also collect basic site analytics (pages visited, referral source, browser type) using standard web analytics tools.

Information submitted through quote forms is shared with the auto transport companies you request quotes from. Analytics data is used in aggregate to improve site content and functionality. We do not sell personal information.
